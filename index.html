directions

Fly

Palm Springs International Airport (PSP) is 40 miles away. LAX is 140 miles away.

Drive

From Palm Springs Airport
Get on the I-10 W and take exit 117 for CA-62 E/29 Palms Hwy towards 29 Palms/Yucca Valley. Soon after you pass by the west entrance to Joshua Tree National Park, you'll see the sign for Mojave Sands Motel on your right (at the junction with Sunburst St).
From LA
Follow I-10 E to Whitewater and take exit 117 for CA-62 E/29 Palms Hwy. Then onwards, follow same directions as from Palm Springs.
From SF
Joshua Tree is 510 miles south-east. Please follow Google Maps directions.
